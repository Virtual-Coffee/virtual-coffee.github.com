## Volunteering at Virtual Coffee

### Get Involved

As you can see, Virtual Coffee has <Link href="/resources/virtual-coffee-handbook/guides-to-virtual-coffee/glossary#coffees">Coffees (Virtual Coffee weekly Zoom chats)</Link>, <Link href="/events">events</Link>, and even <Link href="/resources/virtual-coffee-handbook/guides-to-virtual-coffee/glossary#office-hours">office hours</Link>! And these are all becoming possible thanks to our members who run these voluntarily. Do you want to <Link href="/resources/virtual-coffee-handbook/get-involved">get involved</Link> in Virtual Coffee?

If you want to join the volunteer team, check out <Link href="/resources/virtual-coffee-handbook/get-involved/paths-to-leadership">our open roles</Link>! If you want to share your knowledge with other members through a presentation, consider giving a <Link href="/resources/virtual-coffee-handbook/guides-to-virtual-coffee/lunch-and-learns">Lunch & Learn</Link> session. And if you are interested in starting a new <Link href="/resources/virtual-coffee-handbook/guides-to-virtual-coffee/glossary#coffee-table-groups">Coffee Table group</Link>, you can find more information on the <Link href="/resources/virtual-coffee-handbook/get-involved/leading-coffee-table-groups">Leading a Coffee Table Group</Link> page.

If you have questions about getting involved in Virtual Coffee, drop them in the `#community-questions` channel or contact one of our <Link href="/members">Maintainers</Link>.

## Contributing to Virtual Coffee

### Virtual Coffee's Repositories

Virtual Coffee's [website](https://github.com/Virtual-Coffee/virtualcoffee.io) and [podcast transcripts](https://github.com/Virtual-Coffee/podcast-transcripts) are open source, and we love contributions! Please read our Contributing Guide (CONTRIBUTING.md) in each repository before contributing.

If you still need to become familiar with open source, we got you covered! Head over to our <Link href="/resources/developer-resources/open-source">Open Source Resources</Link> for more guidance about open source. And if you have questions, you can always drop them in the `open-source` channel on Slack.

### Sponsorships

[Sponsorships](https://github.com/sponsors/Virtual-Coffee) help us continue serving the Virtual Coffee community and supporting everything you see on this website!
